Work

Dash MD App by Dash Digital Healthcare

Mobile Developer, CTO, Co-Founder• May 2015 - Present

Dash MD is a medical app that helps patients manage their recovery process after being discharged from the hospital's Emergency Department, it features todo tasks with customizable reminders, care instructions from the hospitals, as well as a clinic catalog that helps patient locate nearest care providers.
As the technical founder of the team, I developed the app natively on both Android and iOS, which are currently live in the Markham Stouffville Hospital in Toronto, ON.

E-Rhythms Project

Android Developer• Dec 2013 - Feb 2016

In the E-Rhythms Project, we developed an Android app that collects usage and survey response data for research use that studies phone-based social communication pattern. As the Android developer of the team, I built the Android app that are installed on research participant's smart phones and collaborated with the web developer to build RESTful web APIs that are used by the app.
